Square Face:

Square faces are strong and angular, with a broad forehead and jaw. Haircuts like crew cuts, fades, and pompadours work well because they highlight the faceâ€™s structure while slightly softening the edges.

Round Face:

A round face is soft and circular, with full cheeks and little definition in the jaw. To add structure, styles such as pompadours, high fades, and side parts create height and sharpness, making the face appear more defined.

Oval Face:

An oval face has balanced proportions with a slightly rounded jawline. Itâ€™s the most versatile shape, meaning almost any haircut works well. Styles like quiffs, buzz cuts, and buzz cuts all complement the natural symmetry of this face shape.

Rectangle Face:

A rectangle face is longer than it is wide, with straight sides and a defined jaw. To avoid making the face look even longer, styles like fringe, textured crops, or side parts help add width and balance out the length.

Buzz Cut

  • Top & Sides: Uniform short length (clipper all over)
  • Style: Clean, simple, and very low maintenance

Fade Cut

  • Top: Short, gradually blended
  • Sides: Skin or low length at the bottom, fading upward
  • Style: Sharp and clean

Crew Cut

  • Top: Short and evenly trimmed
  • Sides: Faded or tapered into the top
  • Style: Neat, low-maintenance

High Fade Cut

  • Top: Short to medium
  • Sides: Fade starts high near the temples
  • Style: Sharp contrast between top and sides

Fringe Cut

  • Top: Medium to long length
  • Front: Hair falls forward over the forehead
  • Sides: Short or faded, but not skin-tight
  • Style: Straight or slightly textured fringe

Pompadour Cut

  • Top: Long with volume, styled upward and back
  • Sides: Short, can be faded or tapered
  • Style: Bold and stylish

Quiff Cut

  • Top: Long at the front, shorter toward the back
  • Style: Hair is brushed upward and slightly back
  • Sides: Short or tapered
  • Look: Voluminous and stylish

Side Part Cut

  • Top: Medium length, combed to the side
  • Part: Defined side part
  • Sides: Tapered or faded for a clean finish
  • Style: Classic, formal

Textured Crop Cut

  • Top: Short, messy, and choppy layers
  • Fringe: Forward-facing and uneven
  • Sides: Faded or tapered
  • Style: Casual and edgy

Waves Cut

  • Top: Maintains natural 360 or ripple wave pattern
  • Sides: Tapered or faded
  • Style: Requires brushing and wave maintenance products
